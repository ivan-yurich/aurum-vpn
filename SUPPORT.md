- Donate: [dzen.ru/ivanyurievich?donate=true](https://dzen.ru/ivanyurievich?donate=true)

When reporting a VPN issue, include:

- app version;
- Android version and device model;
- selected protocol, such as VLESS Reality or NaiveProxy;
- whether the issue happens on Wi-Fi, mobile network, or both;
- the diagnostic report generated by the app.

Do not send real profile passwords, private keys, or full subscription links in
public GitHub issues.
